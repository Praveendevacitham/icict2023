    ABOUT THE CONFERENCE


    INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE - ICICT 2023

         International Conference on Innovation in Computing & Technology (ICICT-2023) is aimed to promote the scientific platform for exchange of ideas between the researches and promoting innovation aspects in Engineering & Science. The conference will boost excellent innovations in terms of day life and academics. This International Conference on Innovation in Computing & Technology is Organized on 31 MARCH 2023 by Velammal Institute of Technology, Chennai, India. This Conference is an initiation to build a technical society for future collaboration and specific goal of globalization to achieve international standards.

    ABOUT THE DEPARTMENTS

    COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING

         The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2008 with a sanctioned strength of 60 and the intake was increased to 180 in 2022. The aim of the department is to create quality computer professionals in the nation’s march towards the digital age. The department is equipped with well established Computing Laboratories to offer training in Internet Technology, Database Applications and Open Software. Apart from imparting conventional, technical, educational and rich learning environment, emphasis is laid on co-curricular activities such as Seminars, Workshops, Guest Lectures, Technical Quizzes and Paper Presentations to prepare students for highly competitive job market. Also the department aims at establishing strong relationship with the industry so as to bridge the gap between academic and corporate sector. The department has been accredited by NBA &NAAC
